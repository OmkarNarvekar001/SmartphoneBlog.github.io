Samsung Galaxy A52 5G Review

As a budget-friendly device, the Samsung Galaxy A52 5G features a plastic back casing with an aluminum frame. It’s a solid device, built to endure those minor bumps and drops. In terms of design, you’ll have difficulty discerning this handset from premium models. The 6.5-inch HD+ Infinity-O Display – featuring a 120Hz refresh rate - beats much pricier phones on the market, and stretches across the front of the device. Completing the minimalist look, you’ll find only the lock key and volume rocker on the side of the device – the fingerprint scanner is fully integrated into the screen.
